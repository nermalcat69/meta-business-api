Get Started

Updated: May 21, 2026
This section will help you get started with your very first (demo) Flow.

Prerequisites

  1. Set up WhatsApp Business Platform⁠
    • This will give you access to the WhatsApp Manager⁠ as part of your WhatsApp Business account.
  2. Set up the WhatsApp Business Platform Cloud API
    • Includes setup of Meta App and phone number
  3. To be able to send Flows to your customers you will need to:
    • Verify your business
    • Maintain a high message quality.
