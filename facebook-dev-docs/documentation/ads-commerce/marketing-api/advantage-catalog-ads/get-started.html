Advantage+ Catalog Ads

Updated: May 21, 2026
With Advantage+ catalog ads⁠, you can promote relevant items from an entire catalog across any device. Display ads for thousands of items to the right audience and automate the process. The solution has three major elements:
  1. Create a feed from your catalog to automatically deliver images, descriptions, and prices in your ads.
  2. Set up App Events or Meta Pixel to measure actions, such as purchases, and profile target audiences for ads.
  3. Create and deliver ads based on your catalog. The ads display relevant items from your catalog feed and target your audience.
After you set up these three elements, you can see how people on Facebook are engaging with your ads and use debugging tools to diagnose and resolve problems.
