# Get Started

Updated: May 21, 2026

This section will help you get started with your very first (demo) Flow.

## Prerequisites

* Set up [WhatsApp Business Platform⁠](https://business.whatsapp.com/products/business-platform)
  * This will give you access to the [WhatsApp Manager⁠](https://business.facebook.com/wa/manage/partner_home/) as part of your WhatsApp Business account.
* [Set up the WhatsApp Business Platform Cloud API](https://developers.facebook.com/documentation/business-messaging/whatsapp/get-started)
  * Includes setup of Meta App and phone number
* To be able to send Flows to your customers you will need to:
  * [Verify your business](https://developers.facebook.com/docs/development/release/business-verification)
  * Maintain a [high message quality](https://developers.facebook.com/documentation/business-messaging/whatsapp/messages/send-messages#message-quality).
