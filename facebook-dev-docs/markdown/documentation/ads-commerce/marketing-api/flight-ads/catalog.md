# Flight ads

Updated: Jun 24, 2026

Flight ads use cross-device signals to automatically promote relevant flights from your inventory with distinct creatives on Facebook.

## How to set up flight ads

* [Create a flight catalog containing your flight inventory](https://developers.facebook.com/documentation/ads-commerce/marketing-api/flight-ads/catalog)
* [Set up flight events on your website and in your app](https://developers.facebook.com/docs/marketing-api/dynamic-ads-for-flights/events)
* [Build audiences to target with your Advantage+ catalog ads](https://developers.facebook.com/documentation/ads-commerce/marketing-api/travel-ads/audience-management)
* [Create and start your flight ads](https://developers.facebook.com/docs/marketing-api/dynamic-ads-for-travel/ads-management)
* [Use flight template tags in your ads](https://developers.facebook.com/documentation/ads-commerce/marketing-api/flight-ads/template-tags)
