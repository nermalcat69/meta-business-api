# Advantage+ Catalog Ads

Updated: May 21, 2026

With [Advantage+ catalog ads⁠](https://www.facebook.com/business/a/online-sales/dynamic-product-ads), you can promote relevant items from an entire catalog across any device. Display ads for thousands of items to the right audience and automate the process. The solution has three major elements:

![Three-step Advantage+ catalog ads diagram: 1 Set up Catalog, 2 Build Target Audience, 3 Create and Deliver Ads from Your Catalog](https://scontent.fdel1-6.fna.fbcdn.net/v/t39.2365-6/23204403_493201307718997_4485178727386316800_n.png?_nc_cat=108&_nc_map=urlgen_bucketless&ccb=1-7&_nc_sid=e280be&_nc_ohc=wxzI4czIfTEQ7kNvwH3E_ZO&_nc_oc=AdoMRLAKwHKIuKYdX3gJ0B9VFeu9_-kefopdOeAzMkNSEB7lh5QE4EORs18_vwQ_b0qes3T69M2jFkFfl82wZz_Y&_nc_zt=14&_nc_ht=scontent.fdel1-6.fna&_nc_gid=XOAa3CrcoU86fmJwXciToQ&_nc_ss=7b289&oh=00_AQDxRvPoTXXMR-YPlzNGI-fT0WSZ76MLON-d49k6S03GjA&oe=6A60568D)

* [Create a feed from your catalog](https://developers.facebook.com/documentation/ads-commerce/catalog) to automatically deliver images, descriptions, and prices in your ads.
* Set up [App Events](https://developers.facebook.com/docs/app-events) or [Meta Pixel](https://developers.facebook.com/docs/meta-pixel) to measure actions, such as purchases, and profile target [audiences](https://developers.facebook.com/documentation/ads-commerce/marketing-api/audiences/guides/dynamic-product-audiences) for ads.
* [Create and deliver ads based on your catalog](https://developers.facebook.com/docs/marketing-api/dynamic-ads/get-started). The ads display relevant items from your catalog feed and target your audience.

After you set up these three elements, you can see how people on Facebook are [engaging with your ads](https://developers.facebook.com/documentation/ads-commerce/marketing-api/reference/adgroup/insights) and use [debugging tools](https://developers.facebook.com/docs/marketing-api/dynamic-product-ads/debugging-tools) to diagnose and resolve problems.
