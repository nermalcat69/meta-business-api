## About App Bidding

App Bidding helps publishers to establish an impartial and open auction over their
ad inventory by offering every ad opportunity to multiple demand sources in real time. Among the benefits:

* Opportunity to optimize for every ad request resulting in more revenue
* Visibility into the true value of inventory
* Easy to maintain with fewer ad ops resources
