# Meta Business SDK

Updated: Apr 25, 2025

The Meta Business SDK gives you access to our suite of business APIs allowing you to build unique and customized solutions to serve your businesses and clients.

## Common Uses

* [Ads Buying](https://developers.facebook.com/documentation/business-sdk/common-scenarios/ads-buying) — Guide for creating ad campaigns for Click to Messenger Ads and promoting your Facebook Page.
* [Instagram Management](https://developers.facebook.com/docs/business-sdk/common-scenarios/instagram-management) — Guide for publishing photos and responding to comments on Instagram.
* [Onboarding Clients at Scale](https://developers.facebook.com/documentation/business-sdk/common-scenarios/onboard-at-scale) — Guide for managing hundreds or thousands of small businesses and offering them ads buying within your website or platform.
* [Page Management](https://developers.facebook.com/docs/business-sdk/common-scenarios/page-management) — Guide to creating pages, updating pages, and managing Page content.
* [Catalog Management](https://developers.facebook.com/documentation/ads-commerce/catalog/get-started/integrate-via-meta-sdk) — Guide to manage products.
